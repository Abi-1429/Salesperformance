# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard is designed to analyze sales performance and provide business insights through interactive visualizations. The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor sales trends, profitability, customer behavior, and regional performance to support data-driven decision making.


## 🎯 Objectives
- Track overall sales and profit performance
- Analyze sales across different regions and categories
- Identify top-performing products
- Monitor monthly sales trends
- Understand customer purchasing patterns

---

## 📈 Key Dashboard Metrics
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Sales by Region
- Sales by Category
- Top Performing Products
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Customer Insights

---
